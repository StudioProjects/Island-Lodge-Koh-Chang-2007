Welcome to Island Lodge

Founded by expatriot Englishman Graham Rivens and his wife Fon in 1993, Island Lodge is a boutique lodge located in the tranquil surrounds of White Sand Beach in Koh Chang Thailand, a mere 3 minute walk from the hustle and bustle of the main street.

Island Lodge can offer a variety of accomodation, from simple thatched huts to comfortable western style rooms with satellite televsion and airconditioning.

Koh Chang Thailand

Virtually unknown to tourists, Koh Chang is steadily becoming one of the major attractions of the Gulf of Thaland. Thai nationals have been holidaying in this unspoilt location for the last decade and the national secret has become the domain of European travellers in the last 3-5 years.
